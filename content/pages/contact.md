## Frequently Asked Questions

**Do I need to commit to long-term tutoring?**
No. We offer flexible arrangements including short-term and trial sessions.

**Can I request a specific tutor?**
If you have a preference, let us know. We'll do our best to accommodate.

**What if the tutor isn't the right fit?**
We'll match you with another tutor. Finding the right fit is important.

**Do you offer online tutoring?**
Yes, we offer online tutoring for all subjects and curricula.

**Which areas in Thailand do you cover?**
We cover Bangkok, Phuket, Chiang Mai, Pattaya, Hua Hin, and most major cities. Online tutoring is available anywhere.
