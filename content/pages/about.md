# About Private Tutoring Thailand

## Who We Are

Private Tutoring Thailand helps families find qualified private tutors across Thailand. We specialize in supporting students in international schools and families using international curricula.

## Our Approach

We believe that the right tutor makes a real difference. Not just any tutor — the right tutor for your child, your curriculum, and your family's situation.

### How We Match Tutors

When you contact us, we take the time to understand:

- Your child's subjects and curriculum
- Their current level and goals
- Any specific learning needs or challenges
- Your preferred schedule and location
- The personality and teaching style that would work best

Based on this, we match you with a tutor who has the right expertise and approach. We then arrange a trial session so you can see if the fit is right before committing.

### What Makes Us Different

- **We know Thailand** — We understand the international school landscape, the curricula, and the challenges faced by families here
- **We know curricula** — Our tutors understand IB, IGCSE, A Level, and other international systems
- **We match carefully** — We don't just send the nearest available tutor
- **We're flexible** — In-person, online, short-term, long-term, and everything in between
- **We communicate** — Regular check-ins and progress updates

## Who We Help

We work with a wide range of families in Thailand:

- Expat families navigating international education
- Relocating families needing transition support
- Homeschool families needing subject expertise
- Families with children who have learning differences
- Families preparing for important exams
- Families seeking short-term tutoring during visits

## Our Promise

- We match the right tutor, not just any tutor
- We understand international curricula
- We respect your family's schedule and privacy
- We communicate clearly and regularly
- We adjust if the fit isn't right
