# About Private Tutoring Thailand

## Who We Are

Private Tutoring Thailand helps families find qualified private tutors across Thailand. We specialize in supporting students in international schools and families using international curricula.

Our service is designed for families who need more than a generic tutor directory. We take the time to understand your child's school, curriculum, and learning style before recommending anyone. This personalized approach has made us a trusted resource for families in Bangkok, Phuket, Chiang Mai, and beyond.

## Our Story

Private Tutoring Thailand was founded to solve a problem that many expat families face: finding a tutor who truly understands international curricula and the local school landscape. Over time, we have built a network of tutors across Thailand who specialize in supporting students in IB, IGCSE, A Level, and other systems. We started by focusing on Bangkok, where the concentration of international schools is highest, and gradually expanded to serve families in Phuket, Chiang Mai, and beyond.

Our growth has been driven by word of mouth. Parents who found the right tutor through us told other parents. Schools recommended us to families who needed extra support. We have learned from every match, refining our process to make tutoring more effective and less stressful for families.

## Our Approach

We believe that the right tutor makes a real difference. Not just any tutor — the right tutor for your child, your curriculum, and your family's situation.

When you contact us, we take the time to understand:

Based on this, we match you with a tutor who has the right expertise and approach. We then arrange a trial session so you can see if the fit is right before committing.

## How We Vet Tutors

We take tutor quality seriously. Our vetting process includes several steps designed to verify expertise and reliability without making claims about specific credentials that we have not independently confirmed.

**Initial screening** — We review each tutor's teaching experience, subject knowledge, and familiarity with international curricula. We ask about the age groups they have taught and the exam boards they know.

**Interview and assessment** — We conduct a structured interview to evaluate communication skills, professionalism, and teaching philosophy. Tutors must demonstrate that they can explain concepts clearly and adapt to different learning styles.

**Reference checks** — We contact previous employers or families the tutor has worked with to confirm reliability, punctuality, and effectiveness.

**Trial period** — New tutors begin with a small number of students. We collect feedback from families and observe how the tutor performs before expanding their workload.

**Ongoing monitoring** — We stay in touch with tutors and families throughout the tutoring relationship. If a tutor's performance declines or schedules become unreliable, we address it immediately.

This process means that not every applicant becomes a tutor in our network. We would rather have fewer, high-quality tutors than a large directory of unverified profiles.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**How do I know if Private Tutoring Thailand is right for my family?**
If your child attends an international school in Thailand, follows an international curriculum, and needs subject-specific support, we are likely a good fit. We specialize in these exact situations.

**Do you only work with expat families?**
No. We work with any family whose child is studying in an international curriculum, including Thai families who have chosen international education for their children.

**How is Private Tutoring Thailand different from a tutoring agency?**
We are not a directory of random tutors. We match carefully, monitor quality, and maintain ongoing relationships with both families and tutors. Our focus is on long-term outcomes, not quick placements.

**What happens if the tutor is not a good fit?**
We will rematch you with another tutor. Finding the right fit is part of our process, and we do not charge extra for rematching.

**Do you offer group tutoring?**
Our primary focus is one-to-one tutoring because it allows for personalized attention. In some cases, we can arrange paired tutoring for siblings or friends studying the same curriculum and level.

**How do you handle privacy?**
We never share your personal information with third parties. Tutors receive only the information they need to prepare for sessions. Communication about your child's progress happens directly between you and the tutor, with our support when needed.

**Can you help with curriculum transitions?**
Yes. We regularly support families who are moving from one curriculum to another, such as from British to IB or from American to Cambridge. Tutors identify gaps and create catch-up plans.
