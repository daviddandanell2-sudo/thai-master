# Private Tutoring in Thailand

Finding the right tutor for your child in Thailand shouldn't be complicated. Whether you're an expat family navigating international curricula, a relocating family needing transition support, or a local family seeking extra academic help, we connect you with qualified private tutors who understand your child's needs.

We specialize in one-to-one tutoring for students in international schools across Thailand. Our tutors know the curricula, the schools, and the challenges that families face when living abroad. Every match is made with care, and every tutor is vetted for subject expertise and professionalism.

Our goal is simple: to help your child succeed academically while reducing the stress that often comes with finding the right educational support in a new country.

## Why Choose Us

We focus on three things that make a real difference for families in Thailand.

**1. Curriculum expertise** — Our tutors understand IB, IGCSE, A Level, Cambridge, British, American, and Australian systems. They do not just know the subject; they know how your child's school assesses it.

**2. Local knowledge** — We understand Thailand's international school landscape. We know which schools follow which curricula, where families live, and how traffic and geography affect tutoring logistics.

**3. Careful matching** — We do not send the nearest available tutor. We match based on subject expertise, curriculum familiarity, location, schedule, and personality fit. A trial session lets you confirm the fit before committing.

## What We Do

Private Tutoring Thailand provides one-to-one tutoring across Thailand for families using international curricula. Our tutors support students in IB, IGCSE, A Level, Cambridge, British, American, and Australian curricula. We offer both in-person tutoring at your home and online tutoring for flexibility.

- **One-to-one private tutoring** — Personalized sessions at your home, villa, or condo. The tutor comes to you, saving travel time and creating a comfortable learning environment.
- **Online tutoring** — Live video sessions for remote or traveling families. Online tutoring uses digital whiteboards, screen sharing, and document collaboration to maintain engagement.
- **Exam preparation** — Structured revision for IGCSE, A Level, IB, SAT, and ACT. Tutors teach exam technique, time management, and content review.
- **SEN and learning support** — Specialized support for students with ADHD, dyslexia, autism spectrum traits, and other learning differences. Tutors adapt pacing and materials to suit the student.
- **Homeschool support** — Curriculum planning, subject delivery, and exam registration guidance for families educating at home.
- **Short-term tutoring** — Intensive support for families visiting Thailand or transitioning between schools. Sessions can be condensed into a short timeframe or spread across a stay.
