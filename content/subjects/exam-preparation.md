## Frequently Asked Questions

**What age should my child start exam preparation tutoring?**
Most exam preparation tutoring begins around age 14-16 when students approach IGCSE or IB. However, younger students can benefit from building good study habits earlier.

**Which curriculum does your exam preparation tutoring follow?**
We follow your child's curriculum, covering the specific syllabus, exam format, and assessment criteria for IB, IGCSE, A Level, or another system.

**Do you offer online exam preparation tutoring?**
Yes, online exam preparation tutoring works well, especially for past paper practice and revision discussions.

**How do I know if my child needs exam preparation tutoring?**
Signs include anxiety about upcoming exams, poor performance in mocks, lack of a revision plan, or a significant gap between classwork and exam results.

**Can a tutor help with exam stress and anxiety?**
Yes, tutors can help students develop coping strategies, build confidence through practice, and approach exams with a calmer mindset.

**How long are exam preparation tutoring sessions?**
Sessions are typically one hour. Some students benefit from longer sessions during intensive revision periods.

**What should my child bring to an exam preparation tutoring session?**
Past papers, school notes, syllabus documents, a list of weak topics, and any mock exam feedback.
