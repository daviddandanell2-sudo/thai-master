## How Math Tutors Help

A good math tutor does more than explain formulas. They:

- Identify knowledge gaps and fill them systematically
- Teach problem-solving strategies, not just procedures
- Build exam technique and time management
- Increase confidence through structured practice
- Adapt to the student's learning pace and style

## Math Exam Preparation

For students approaching exams, tutoring focuses on:

- Past paper practice under timed conditions
- Common mistake patterns and how to avoid them
- Revision of weak topics
- Exam strategy and time allocation
- Building confidence for exam day

## Frequently Asked Questions

**What age should my child start math tutoring?**
There's no fixed age. Some students need support from primary school, others only when reaching IGCSE or IB. The right time is when your child is struggling, losing confidence, or preparing for important exams.

**Which curriculum does your math tutoring follow?**
We follow your child's curriculum — whether that's IB, IGCSE, A Level, Cambridge, British, or American.

**Do you offer online math tutoring?**
Yes, online math tutoring works well, especially with digital whiteboards and screen sharing.

**How do I know if my child needs a math tutor?**
Signs include declining grades, homework struggles, exam anxiety, avoidance of math, or teacher feedback about gaps.

**Can a tutor help with math exam preparation?**
Absolutely. Exam-focused tutoring is one of our most requested services.
