## Frequently Asked Questions

**What age should my child start writing support tutoring?**
Any age from 8 upwards. Younger students benefit from grammar and sentence-building support, while older students often need essay and exam writing help.

**Which curriculum does your writing tutoring follow?**
We follow your child's curriculum, whether that's IB, IGCSE, A Level, Cambridge, British, or American.

**Do you offer online writing tutoring?**
Yes, online writing tutoring works very well. Tutors can review drafts, provide feedback, and guide the writing process through shared documents.

**How do I know if my child needs a writing tutor?**
Signs include difficulty starting assignments, teacher feedback about writing quality, lost marks on grammar, or stress about essay deadlines.

**Can a tutor help with the IB Extended Essay?**
Yes, tutors can guide students through the Extended Essay process, from topic selection to final editing, while ensuring the work remains the student's own.

**How long are writing tutoring sessions?**
Sessions are typically one hour. Extended Essay support sessions may sometimes run longer by arrangement.

**What should my child bring to a writing tutoring session?**
Current writing assignments, school English books, any drafts or notes, and a laptop or notebook.
