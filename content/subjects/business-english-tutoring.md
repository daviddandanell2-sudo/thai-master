**What age should my child start Business English tutoring?**
Business English tutoring is typically for students aged 16-18 who are preparing for professional contexts, university business programs, or workplace internships.

**Which curriculum does your Business English tutoring follow?**
We adapt to the student's needs, whether that's support for school business or economics courses, university preparation, or general professional English development.

**Do you offer online Business English tutoring?**
Yes, online Business English tutoring works well, especially for presentation practice and writing feedback.

**How do I know if my child needs a Business English tutor?**
Signs include difficulty with business-related writing, anxiety about presentations, plans to study business at university, or preparation for English-speaking work environments.

**Can a tutor help with interview preparation?**
Yes, tutors can conduct mock interviews, help refine answers, and build confidence for university or job interviews in English.

**How long are Business English tutoring sessions?**
Sessions are typically one hour. Presentation practice sessions may sometimes be longer.

**What should my child bring to a Business English tutoring session?**
School business or English assignments, any presentation materials, a notebook, and specific goals for what they want to improve.
