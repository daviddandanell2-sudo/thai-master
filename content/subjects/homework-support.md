## Frequently Asked Questions

**What age should my child start homework support tutoring?**
Homework support tutoring is helpful from age 6 upwards. Younger students benefit from building routines, while older students often need help managing heavier workloads.

**Which curriculum does your homework support tutoring follow?**
We follow your child's curriculum, adapting to the subjects, assignments, and expectations of their school.

**Do you offer online homework support tutoring?**
Yes, online homework support works well. Students can share screens for assignments and work through problems in real time.

**How do I know if my child needs homework support tutoring?**
Signs include homework taking excessive time, frequent frustration, missed deadlines, or difficulty understanding assignments.

**Can a tutor help with all subjects or just one?**
Homework support tutors typically help across subjects, though they may recommend subject-specific tutoring if a particular area needs deeper focus.

**How long are homework support tutoring sessions?**
Sessions are typically one hour. Some students benefit from longer sessions when they have multiple assignments or projects.

**What should my child bring to a homework support tutoring session?**
All current homework assignments, school textbooks and notes, a planner or assignment list, and any materials needed for the work.
