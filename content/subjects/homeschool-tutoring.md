## Frequently Asked Questions

**What age should my child start homeschool tutoring?**
Homeschool tutoring is available for any age from 5 to 18. Some families use tutors for specific subjects from the start; others bring in tutors for exam preparation in the teen years.

**Which curriculum does your homeschool tutoring follow?**
We follow your chosen curriculum, whether that's a formal syllabus like IGCSE or A Level, or a more flexible homeschool approach.

**Do you offer online homeschool tutoring?**
Yes, online homeschool tutoring works very well and offers flexibility for traveling families or those in remote locations.

**How do I know if my child needs a homeschool tutor?**
Signs include struggling with certain subjects, approaching exam years, needing expert input beyond what parents can provide, or wanting more structure in the homeschool routine.

**Can a tutor help with exam registration?**
Tutors can provide guidance on exam boards, syllabi, and registration processes, though families typically handle the actual registration directly with exam centers.

**How long are homeschool tutoring sessions?**
Sessions are typically one to two hours, depending on the subject and the child's age and concentration span.

**What should my child bring to a homeschool tutoring session?**
Curriculum materials, any textbooks or resources, current assignments, a notebook, and a list of topics or questions to focus on.
