## Frequently Asked Questions

**What age should my child start English tutoring?**
Any age. Younger students often need reading support, while older students need essay and exam help. EAL students may need ongoing support.

**Which curriculum does your English tutoring follow?**
We follow your child's curriculum, whether IB, IGCSE, A Level, or another system.

**Do you offer online English tutoring?**
Yes, online English tutoring works very well, especially for writing feedback and discussion.

**How do I know if my child needs an English tutor?**
Signs include difficulty with writing assignments, low reading comprehension scores, teacher feedback about language skills, or anxiety about English exams.

**Can a tutor help with English exam preparation?**
Yes, exam preparation is a core part of our English tutoring, including past paper practice and essay technique.
