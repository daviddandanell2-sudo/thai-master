# Science Tutoring in Thailand

Science tutoring covers biology, chemistry, and physics — the three core sciences that students encounter in international curricula. Many students excel in one science but struggle with another, and tutoring can help balance skills and build confidence across all three.

## Why Parents Seek Science Tutoring

Parents often tell us:

- "My child understands biology but struggles with chemistry"
- "The physics concepts are abstract and hard to visualize"
- "Lab reports take too long and don't get good marks"
- "There's too much content to revise for science exams"
- "My child needs help connecting theory to real-world examples"

## Frequently Asked Questions

**What age should my child start science tutoring?**
Most science tutoring begins around age 11-12 when students start separate sciences, though some need support earlier in combined science courses.

**Which curriculum does your science tutoring follow?**
We follow your child's curriculum, covering the specific syllabus for IB, IGCSE, A Level, or another system.

**Do you offer online science tutoring?**
Yes, online science tutoring works well with digital whiteboards for diagrams and calculations.

**How do I know if my child needs a science tutor?**
Signs include difficulty with science homework, low test scores, anxiety about lab work, or teacher feedback about understanding gaps.

**Can a tutor help with science exam preparation?**
Yes, exam-focused science tutoring is one of our most requested services, especially for IGCSE and IB.
