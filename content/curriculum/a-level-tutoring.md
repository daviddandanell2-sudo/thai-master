# A Level Tutoring in Thailand

A Level (Advanced Level) is the qualification that determines university admissions for many students in British and international education systems. Taken over two years (ages 16-18), A Levels require deep subject knowledge, strong analytical skills, and effective exam technique. For students in Thailand, finding a tutor who understands the demands of A Level study can be the difference between meeting and missing university offers.

## What Is A Level?

A Level is a two-year program:

- **Year 1 (AS Level)** — Students typically take 4 subjects. Exams at the end of Year 1 contribute to the final A Level grade (in most syllabi).
- **Year 2 (A2 Level)** — Students usually continue with 3 subjects. Final exams at the end of Year 2 determine the A Level grade.

Key features:

- **Deep focus** — Students study only 3-4 subjects, but in significant depth
- **External exams** — Set by Cambridge, Edexcel, AQA, or OCR
- **University entrance** — A Level grades are the primary basis for UK university admissions
- **Rigorous standard** — A Level is known for its academic challenge
