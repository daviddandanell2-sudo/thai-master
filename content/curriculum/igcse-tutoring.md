# IGCSE Tutoring in Thailand

IGCSE (International General Certificate of Secondary Education) is one of the most widely taken qualifications by international school students in Thailand. The two-year program culminates in external exams that significantly influence a student's academic future. For many families, IGCSE tutoring is essential to help their child perform at their best.

## What Is IGCSE?

IGCSE is a two-year program typically taken by students aged 14-16 (Years 10-11). It is set and assessed by Cambridge International or Edexcel, and is recognized by universities and employers worldwide.

Key features of IGCSE:

- **Core and Extended levels** — Core is designed for grades C-G. Extended is designed for grades A*-E. Schools and students choose the appropriate level.
- **Wide subject range** — Over 70 subjects available, though most students take 8-10
- **External assessment** — Final exams are set by Cambridge or Edexcel and marked externally
- **No coursework** — Most IGCSE subjects are assessed entirely by exam, though some include coursework options

## Why Families in Thailand Choose IGCSE

- **British qualification** — Recognized in the UK and Commonwealth countries
- **Pathway to A Level** — IGCSE is the standard preparation for A Level studies
- **International schools** — Many of Thailand's British and international schools offer IGCSE, including Harrow, Shrewsbury, and St. Andrews
- **Clear structure** — Two-year program with defined syllabus and exam dates
