# International School Tutoring in Thailand

International schools in Thailand offer a range of curricula, from British and American to IB and Cambridge. While these schools provide excellent education, many students need extra support outside the classroom to keep up, catch up, or excel. Our tutors work with students from all international schools, providing personalized one-to-one support that complements what they learn at school.

## What Is International School Tutoring?

International school tutoring is flexible academic support tailored to a student's specific school, curriculum, and needs:

- **Curriculum-specific** — Tutors work within the framework of the student's school curriculum, whether British, American, IB, or Cambridge
- **Subject-focused** — Support for any subject the student is studying
- **Assessment-aligned** — Help with school assessments, projects, and external exams
- **Flexible timing** — Sessions scheduled around the student's school day and extracurricular activities

Key features:

- **School-specific knowledge** — Tutors understand the programs and expectations of major international schools in Thailand
- **One-to-one attention** — Focus entirely on the student's questions and weak areas
- **Homework support** — Help understanding and completing school assignments
- **Exam readiness** — Preparation for school exams and external qualifications

## Why Families in Thailand Choose International School Tutoring

- **Individual attention** — Even good schools cannot give every student one-to-one time
- **Curriculum transitions** — Students moving between schools or curricula often need catch-up support
- **Exam pressure** — External exams like IGCSE, A Level, and IB require extra preparation
- **Subject gaps** — A student may fall behind in one subject while doing well in others
- **Confidence building** — Personalized support helps students feel more confident in class
