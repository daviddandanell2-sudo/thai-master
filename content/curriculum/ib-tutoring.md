## What Is the IB?

The IB offers three programs:

- **PYP (Primary Years Programme)** — Ages 3-12. Inquiry-based learning focusing on concepts, knowledge, skills, and action.
- **MYP (Middle Years Programme)** — Ages 11-16. Broad curriculum with increasing depth. Includes the Personal Project.
- **DP (Diploma Programme)** — Ages 16-19. Six subjects, plus Theory of Knowledge (TOK), the Extended Essay (EE), and CAS (Creativity, Activity, Service).

The DP is particularly challenging. Students must balance six subjects, complete a 4,000-word research essay, engage in creative and physical activities, and reflect on the nature of knowledge itself.
