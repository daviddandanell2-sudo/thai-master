# Cambridge Curriculum Tutoring in Thailand

The Cambridge curriculum is one of the most structured and widely recognized educational pathways in the world. ## What Is the Cambridge Curriculum?

Cambridge Assessment International Education offers a continuous pathway for students:

- **Cambridge Primary** — Ages 5-11. Covers English, Mathematics, and Science with optional testing through Cambridge Primary Checkpoint.
- **Cambridge Lower Secondary** — Ages 11-14. Builds on primary foundations with Checkpoint exams at the end to assess readiness for IGCSE.
- **Cambridge IGCSE** — Ages 14-16. The world's most popular international qualification, with over 70 subjects available.
- **Cambridge International A Level** — Ages 16-19. Deep, focused study in 3-4 subjects, preparing students for university.

Key features of the Cambridge curriculum:

- **Structured progression** — Each stage builds clearly on the previous one
- **Global recognition** — Accepted by universities and employers worldwide
- **Flexible subject choices** — Students can tailor their program to their strengths and interests
- **External assessment** — Exams are set and marked by Cambridge, ensuring consistent standards

## Why Families in Thailand Choose the Cambridge Curriculum
