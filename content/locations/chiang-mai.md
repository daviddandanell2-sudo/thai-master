# Private Tutoring in Chiang Mai

Chiang Mai has become a hub for international families, digital nomads, and homeschoolers. With schools like Prem Tinsulanonda International School, Chiang Mai International School, and Lanna International School, the city offers strong international education options — and families here increasingly need tutoring support.

## Why Chiang Mai Matters for Tutoring

Chiang Mai's international school community is diverse. You'll find long-term expat families, digital nomad families who base themselves here for months at a time, and local Thai families choosing international education. The city's relaxed pace and lower cost of living attract families who want quality education without the intensity of Bangkok.

The homeschool community in Chiang Mai is also significant. Many families educate at home and need subject-specific tutors to fill gaps or provide expertise in areas like math, science, or exam preparation.

Chiang Mai is home to several well-regarded international schools. Prem Tinsulanonda International School offers a full IB program in a boarding environment. Chiang Mai International School and Lanna International School serve a mix of expat and local families with American and British curricula. American Pacific International School and Grace International School add further options for families seeking faith-based or alternative approaches. Each school has its own culture, assessment methods, and academic priorities, which tutors must understand to be effective.

The city's geography also shapes tutoring needs. Families in Nimman enjoy easy access to city-center tutors, while those in Mae Rim, Hang Dong, or San Kamphaeng may prefer online sessions or tutors willing to travel. Digital nomad families often have non-traditional schedules, requiring early morning or evening sessions. We accommodate these variations by matching tutors who live close to you or who are comfortable with flexible online arrangements.
