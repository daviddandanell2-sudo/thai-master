# Brand Voice

## Voice Adjectives

The brand voice of Private Tutoring Thailand is:

1. **Clear** — No jargon. No education-speak. Parents should understand immediately.
2. **Professional** — We know what we're doing. We take tutoring seriously.
3. **Parent-friendly** — We speak to parents, not at them. We understand their concerns.
4. **Trustworthy** — We say what we mean. We don't overpromise.
5. **Direct** — Get to the point. Respect the parent's time.
6. **Calm** — Parenting is stressful. We don't add to the stress.
7. **International** — We understand expat life, international schools, and global families.
8. **Helpful** — Every page should answer a question or solve a problem.
9. **Not childish** — We respect children's intelligence without talking down.
10. **Not corporate nonsense** — No meaningless mission statements. No buzzwords.
11. **Not overpromising** — We don't guarantee grades. We guarantee effort and expertise.

## Parent Feelings

The writing should make parents feel:

1. **This company understands international families** — We know what it's like to move countries, change schools, and worry about your child's education.
2. **This company understands tutoring** — We know curricula, exams, and how children learn.
3. **This company understands Thailand** — We know the schools, the cities, and the expat community.
4. **This company can match the right tutor** — We don't just send anyone. We match carefully.
5. **This company is serious about quality** — We vet tutors, check progress, and care about outcomes.

## Writing Rules

### Sentence Structure
- Use short sentences. Aim for 15-20 words on average.
- Use short paragraphs. 2-4 sentences per paragraph.
- Use active voice. "We match tutors" not "Tutors are matched by us."
- Use "you" and "we." Speak directly to the parent.

### Vocabulary Do
- Use plain English that any parent can understand
- Use specific terms for curricula (IB, IGCSE, A Level) — parents know these
- Use location names naturally
- Use action verbs: "find," "match," "support," "help," "prepare"

### Vocabulary Don't
- Don't use education jargon ("pedagogy," "differentiated instruction," "scaffolding")
- Don't use corporate buzzwords ("synergy," "holistic," "paradigm")
- Don't use vague superlatives ("best," "top," "number one") without evidence
- Don't use fear-based language ("your child will fail without tutoring")

### Tone by Page Type

**Home Page:** Warm, welcoming, confident. "We help families find the right tutor in Thailand."

**Location Pages:** Local expertise. "Bangkok has over 50 international schools. We know the tutors who understand these curricula."

**Subject Pages:** Knowledgeable, reassuring. "Math can be challenging. The right tutor makes it manageable."

**Curriculum Pages:** Expert, clarifying. "IGCSE is a two-year program. Here's how tutoring helps."

**Blog Articles:** Helpful, informative. "Moving to Thailand with school-age children? Here's what to know."

**Contact Page:** Easy, low-pressure. "Ask a question. No commitment."

## Trust Signal Patterns

### What Builds Trust
- Specificity: "We work with families in Sukhumvit, Thong Lor, and Sathorn" not "We work everywhere"
- Process: "Here's how we match tutors" not "We have the best tutors"
- Honesty: "Some subjects need more preparation than others" not "We guarantee A grades"
- Local knowledge: "Bangkok's international schools use a mix of British, American, and IB curricula"

### What Destroys Trust
- Fake testimonials
- Fake credentials
- Fake statistics ("95% of our students get A grades")
- Fake urgency ("Only 2 tutors left in Bangkok!")
- Vague claims ("We are the best")

## Examples

### Good Copy
> "Finding the right tutor in Bangkok takes more than a postcode search. You need someone who understands your child's curriculum, learning style, and personality. We match tutors to families — not the other way around."

### Bad Copy
> "We are the premier provider of holistic tutoring solutions in the Thailand educational landscape, leveraging our synergistic network of world-class educators to deliver paradigm-shifting academic outcomes for your cherished offspring."

### Good Headline
> "Private Tutors in Bangkok Who Understand International Curricula"

### Bad Headline
> "The Best Tutors in Thailand — Guaranteed Results!"
