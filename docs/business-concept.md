# Business Concept

## What Private Tutoring Thailand Does

Private Tutoring Thailand connects families with qualified private tutors across Thailand. The service is built for international families, expat parents, and local families using international curricula who need reliable, professional tutoring support.

## Core Services

### One-to-One Private Tutoring
- In-home tutoring at the family's residence, villa, or condo
- Online tutoring via video call for remote or traveling families
- Subject-specific tutoring matched to the student's level and curriculum

### International Curriculum Support
- IB (International Baccalaureate) — PYP, MYP, DP
- IGCSE (Cambridge International)
- A Level (Cambridge, Edexcel, AQA)
- Cambridge Primary and Lower Secondary
- British National Curriculum
- American Curriculum (Common Core, AP)
- Australian Curriculum

### Exam Preparation
- IGCSE exam preparation
- A Level exam preparation
- IB exam preparation
- Entrance exam preparation for international schools
- SAT/ACT preparation for US university applications

### Specialized Support
- SEN (Special Educational Needs) tutoring
- ADHD support strategies
- Dyslexia support
- English as an Additional Language (EAL)
- Thai language tutoring for expat families
- Homeschool curriculum planning and delivery
- Study skills and exam technique coaching

### Short-Term and Relocation Support
- Temporary tutoring for families visiting Thailand
- Transition support for families relocating to Thailand
- Catch-up tutoring for students changing schools or curricula
- Summer tutoring programs

## Service Tiers

### Standard Tutoring
- Qualified tutor matched to subject and level
- Flexible scheduling
- Progress updates to parents
- Suitable for ongoing academic support

### Premium Tutoring
- Highly experienced tutor with curriculum-specific expertise
- Detailed progress reporting
- Customized learning plans
- Suitable for exam preparation and challenging subjects

### SEN and Learning Support
- Specialist tutor with SEN training
- Personalized learning strategies
- Collaboration with parents and school SEN coordinators
- Suitable for students with dyslexia, ADHD, autism spectrum, and other learning differences

## Tutor Matching Process

1. **Parent Inquiry** — Parent submits inquiry via WhatsApp or contact form
2. **Needs Assessment** — Discussion of student's subjects, curriculum, level, goals, and any special requirements
3. **Tutor Selection** — Matching from vetted tutor network based on subject expertise, curriculum knowledge, location, and personality fit
4. **Introduction** — Parent receives tutor profile and background
5. **First Session** — Trial session arranged to confirm fit
6. **Ongoing Support** — Regular check-ins, progress updates, and flexibility to change tutor if needed

- **Thailand-focused** — Deep understanding of international schools, curricula, and family needs in Thailand
- **Expat-specialist** — Experience with relocating families, third-culture kids, and curriculum transitions
