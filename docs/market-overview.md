# Market Overview

## Thailand Tutoring Market

Thailand has a large and growing private tutoring market driven by international schools, expat families, and local families seeking international education. The market is concentrated in major cities with international school presence.

## Primary Locations

### Bangkok
- **Region:** Central Thailand
- **International Schools:** 50+ (highest density in Thailand)
- **Expat Density:** High
- **Tutoring Demand:** High
- **Key Areas:** Sukhumvit, Sathorn, Silom, Thong Lor, Ekkamai, Ari
- **Notes:** Largest market. Diverse curricula. High competition. Families expect premium service.

### Phuket
- **Region:** Southern Thailand
- **International Schools:** 10+
- **Expat Density:** High
- **Tutoring Demand:** High
- **Key Areas:** Laguna, Bang Tao, Rawai, Chalong
- **Notes:** Strong seasonal demand. Many relocating families. Mix of long-term residents and short-stay families.

### Chiang Mai
- **Region:** Northern Thailand
- **International Schools:** 10+
- **Expat Density:** High
- **Tutoring Demand:** Medium-High
- **Key Areas:** Nimman, Hang Dong, Mae Rim
- **Notes:** Digital nomad families. Homeschool community. Growing international school sector.

### Pattaya
- **Region:** Eastern Thailand
- **International Schools:** 5+
- **Expat Density:** Medium
- **Tutoring Demand:** Medium
- **Notes:** Russian and European expat families. Retirement community with grandchildren.

### Hua Hin
- **Region:** Western Thailand (Gulf Coast)
- **International Schools:** 5+
- **Expat Density:** Medium
- **Tutoring Demand:** Medium
- **Notes:** Retirement and second-home families. Growing international school presence.

### Koh Samui
- **Region:** Southern Thailand (Gulf)
- **International Schools:** 3+
- **Expat Density:** Medium
- **Tutoring Demand:** Medium
- **Notes:** Smaller market. Seasonal demand. Families seeking lifestyle + education balance.

### Krabi
- **Region:** Southern Thailand
- **International Schools:** 2+
- **Expat Density:** Low-Medium
- **Tutoring Demand:** Low-Medium
- **Notes:** Emerging market. Fewer international schools. More online tutoring demand.

### Nonthaburi
- **Region:** Greater Bangkok
- **International Schools:** 5+
- **Expat Density:** Medium
- **Tutoring Demand:** Medium
- **Notes:** Bangkok overflow. Families seeking lower cost of living with Bangkok school access.

### Chiang Rai
- **Region:** Northern Thailand
- **International Schools:** 2+
- **Expat Density:** Low-Medium
- **Tutoring Demand:** Low-Medium
- **Notes:** Smaller expat community. Boarding school families.

### Rayong
- **Region:** Eastern Thailand
- **International Schools:** 3+
- **Expat Density:** Medium
- **Tutoring Demand:** Medium
- **Notes:** Industrial expat community. Japanese and European factory families.

### Chonburi
- **Region:** Eastern Thailand
- **International Schools:** 4+
- **Expat Density:** Medium
- **Tutoring Demand:** Medium
- **Notes:** Industrial zone. Mixed local and expat demand.

### Udon Thani

- **International Schools:** 1-2
- **Expat Density:** Low
- **Tutoring Demand:** Low

### Khon Kaen
- **Region:** Northeastern Thailand (Isan)
- **International Schools:** 1-2
- **Expat Density:** Low
- **Tutoring Demand:** Low
- **Notes:** University town. Some academic family demand.

## Parent Personas

### 1. Expat Families Living in Thailand
- **Pain Points:** Curriculum gaps, finding trusted tutors, language barriers, understanding local education system
- **Search Behavior:** "private tutor Bangkok," "IGCSE tutor Phuket," "English tutor Thailand"
- **Decision Criteria:** Tutor qualifications, curriculum experience, reliability, references
- **Content Preferences:** Location guides, curriculum explanations, tutor vetting process

### 2. International School Families
- **Pain Points:** Keeping up with demanding curriculum, exam pressure, subject weaknesses
- **Search Behavior:** "IB math tutor," "IGCSE science tutor," "A Level tutor Bangkok"
- **Decision Criteria:** Curriculum expertise, exam success track record, subject knowledge
- **Content Preferences:** Subject-specific help, exam preparation guides, curriculum comparisons

### 3. Relocating Families
- **Pain Points:** Curriculum transition, catching up, finding schools, understanding options
- **Search Behavior:** "tutoring for relocating families," "homeschool tutor Thailand," "international school tutor"
- **Decision Criteria:** Flexibility, transition support, understanding of multiple curricula
- **Content Preferences:** Relocation guides, curriculum transition advice, city education overviews

### 4. Digital Nomad Families
- **Pain Points:** Inconsistent schooling, maintaining education while traveling, online tutoring needs
- **Search Behavior:** "online tutor Thailand," "homeschool support Thailand," "flexible tutoring"
- **Decision Criteria:** Online capability, flexibility, understanding of non-traditional schooling
- **Content Preferences:** Online tutoring guides, homeschool curriculum advice, flexible scheduling

### 5. Homeschool Families
- **Pain Points:** Curriculum planning, exam registration, subject expertise gaps, socialization concerns
- **Search Behavior:** "homeschool tutor Thailand," "homeschool support Bangkok," "private tutor homeschool"
- **Decision Criteria:** Curriculum knowledge, flexibility, ability to work with homeschool structure
- **Content Preferences:** Homeschool curriculum guides, exam registration help, subject support

### 6. Local Thai Families Using International Curricula
- **Pain Points:** English language support, understanding international systems, exam preparation
- **Search Behavior:** "English tutor Bangkok," "IGCSE tutor Thailand," "international curriculum tutor"
- **Decision Criteria:** English proficiency, curriculum knowledge, cultural understanding
- **Content Preferences:** Curriculum explanations, English language support, local context

### 7. Families Needing English-Speaking Tutors
- **Pain Points:** Finding native or fluent English tutors, subject-specific English support
- **Search Behavior:** "English speaking tutor Thailand," "native English tutor Bangkok," "English math tutor"
- **Decision Criteria:** English fluency, subject expertise, teaching experience
- **Content Preferences:** Tutor profiles, English language support, subject-specific content

### 8. Families Needing Exam Preparation
- **Pain Points:** Exam anxiety, gaps in knowledge, time pressure, understanding exam format
- **Search Behavior:** "IGCSE exam tutor," "IB exam preparation," "A Level revision tutor"
- **Decision Criteria:** Exam-specific experience, past paper knowledge, revision techniques
- **Content Preferences:** Exam preparation guides, past paper resources, revision strategies

### 9. Families Needing SEN or ADHD Support
- **Pain Points:** Finding specialist tutors, understanding SEN needs, school coordination
- **Search Behavior:** "SEN tutor Thailand," "ADHD tutor Bangkok," "dyslexia tutor Thailand"
- **Decision Criteria:** SEN training, patience, customized approach, experience with specific needs
- **Content Preferences:** SEN support guides, tutor qualifications, success stories

### 10. Short-Stay Families Needing Temporary Tutoring
- **Pain Points:** Quick setup, short-term availability, maintaining school progress while traveling
- **Search Behavior:** "short term tutor Thailand," "vacation tutor Phuket," "temporary tutor Bangkok"
- **Decision Criteria:** Speed of setup, flexibility, short-term commitment
- **Content Preferences:** Quick-start guides, location-specific info, flexible scheduling

## Competitor Landscape

The Thailand tutoring market includes:
- Large tutoring centers (Kumon, etc.) — focused on local curriculum, not international
- Individual tutors on marketplace platforms — inconsistent quality, no vetting
- School-recommended tutors — limited availability, narrow subject range
- Online tutoring platforms — generic, not Thailand-specific

**Gap:** Professional, curriculum-specific, Thailand-focused private tutoring with vetting and matching.
