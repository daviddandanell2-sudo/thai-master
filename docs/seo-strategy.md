# SEO Strategy

## Search Layer Architecture

The SEO strategy targets four layers of search intent, from broad to specific:

### Layer 1: National Keywords
Broad searches from parents who know they need a tutor in Thailand but haven't chosen a location.

- **Examples:** private tutor Thailand, tutoring Thailand, online tutor Thailand, homeschool tutor Thailand
- **Intent:** Research and comparison
- **Target Pages:** Home page, national service pages
- **Volume:** High
- **Competition:** Medium

### Layer 2: City Keywords
Location-specific searches from parents who know where they are or where they are moving.

- **Examples:** private tutor Bangkok, tutor Phuket, tutor Chiang Mai, tutoring Pattaya
- **Intent:** Location-based service search
- **Target Pages:** Location pages
- **Volume:** Medium-High
- **Competition:** Medium

### Layer 3: Subject Keywords
Subject-specific searches from parents who know what their child needs help with.

- **Examples:** math tutor Thailand, English tutor Bangkok, science tutor Phuket, physics tutor Chiang Mai
- **Intent:** Subject-specific service search
- **Target Pages:** Subject pages, location-subject combination pages
- **Volume:** Medium
- **Competition:** Low-Medium

### Layer 4: Curriculum Keywords
Curriculum-specific searches from parents using or considering international curricula.

- **Examples:** IB tutor Bangkok, IGCSE tutor Thailand, A Level tutor Phuket, Cambridge tutor Chiang Mai
- **Intent:** Curriculum-specific service search
- **Target Pages:** Curriculum pages, location-curriculum combination pages
- **Volume:** Medium
- **Competition:** Low-Medium

## Keyword Taxonomy

### By Intent Type

**Informational** — Parent is researching, not yet ready to book
- "What is IGCSE?"
- "Best international schools in Bangkok"
- "How to find a tutor in Phuket"
- Target: Blog articles, parent guides

**Transactional** — Parent is ready to contact or book
- "Private tutor Bangkok"
- "IB math tutor Phuket"
- "Hire English tutor Chiang Mai"
- Target: Service pages, location pages, contact page

**Navigational** — Parent is looking for a specific service or brand
- "Private Tutoring Thailand contact"
- "Book tutor consultation"
- Target: Contact page, about page

### By Parent Problem

**Curriculum confusion**
- "IGCSE vs A Level"
- "IB vs British curriculum"
- Target: Curriculum comparison guides

**Relocation stress**
- "Moving to Bangkok schools"
- "Tutoring for relocating families"
- Target: Relocation guides, city education pages

**Exam anxiety**
- "IGCSE exam preparation"
- "IB exam tutor"
- Target: Exam prep pages, subject pages

**Learning differences**
- "ADHD tutor Thailand"
- "SEN support Bangkok"
- Target: SEN support pages

## Internal Linking Architecture

### Hub and Spoke Model

**National Hub:** Home page
- Links to all active location pages
- Links to all active subject pages
- Links to all active curriculum pages
- Links to key blog articles

**Location Hubs:** Location pages
- Links to subject pages ("Math tutoring in Bangkok")
- Links to curriculum pages ("IB tutoring in Bangkok")
- Links to related location-subject pages
- Links to relevant blog articles

**Subject Hubs:** Subject pages
- Links to location pages ("Math tutor Bangkok")
- Links to curriculum pages ("IGCSE math tutor")
- Links to related blog articles

**Curriculum Hubs:** Curriculum pages
- Links to location pages ("IB tutor Bangkok")
- Links to subject pages ("IB math tutor")
- Links to related blog articles

### Link Rules
- Every service page links to at least 3 related pages
- Every blog article links to at least 1 service page
- Anchor text must be descriptive (not "click here")
- No orphaned pages

## Long-Term Coverage Goals

**Phase 1:** National + top 3 cities (Bangkok, Phuket, Chiang Mai) + core subjects (math, English, science) + core curricula (IB, IGCSE, A Level)

**Phase 2:** Expand to all 13 locations + all subjects + all curricula

**Phase 3:** Location-subject combinations for high-demand pairings

**Phase 4:** Blog system covering parent guides, school guides, curriculum guides, relocation guides

**Phase 5:** Ongoing expansion based on search data and lead quality

## URL Structure Rules

- Use lowercase
- Use hyphens, not underscores
- Keep URLs short and descriptive
- Include primary keyword
- No unnecessary words (a, the, and, in)

**Examples:**
- `/locations/bangkok/` (not `/location/bangkok-tutoring/`)
- `/subjects/math-tutoring/` (not `/subject/math-tutor-thailand/`)
- `/curriculum/ib-tutoring/` (not `/curricula/ib-tutor/`)
- `/thailand/bangkok/math-tutor/` (not `/bangkok/math-tutoring-bangkok/`)
