## 1. The 3 Strategic Bets

These three claims are not generic positioning statements. They are specific, evidence-based, searchable competitive advantages validated by competitor gap analysis, keyword research, and market data. Every tactic in this plan serves at least one of these bets.

---

### Bet 1: "The Only Tutoring Service with Vetted In-Person Tutors in 13 Thai Cities"

**Evidence:**
- TutorChase (ranks #1 for "best tutoring companies Thailand") is **online-only** — they have zero physical tutors in Thailand
- Tutopiya (price undercutter at ~$9/hr) is **online-only** — based in Singapore, all tutors remote
- Krutoo is the only Thailand-based physical competitor but covers **Bangkok only** with no multi-city presence
- Keyword research confirms demand for physical tutors in Hua Hin, Koh Samui, Krabi, Chiang Rai, Udon Thani, Khon Kaen, Rayong — **all uncontested**
- 249 international schools exist across Thailand; only Bangkok has meaningful tutoring competition

**What winning looks like:** Own page 1 rankings for "tutor [city]" in every city outside Bangkok within 4 months. Capture relocating families who specifically search for in-person support before they arrive.

**How this plan delivers it:** Build 13 location hub pages in Month 1-2. Build combination pages for each city x top 5 subjects in Month 3-4. Add local school references, curriculum context, and city-specific FAQs to every page to pass Google's doorway-page test.

---

### Bet 2: "Curriculum Specialists for IB, IGCSE & A-Level — Not a Tutor Marketplace"

**Evidence:**
- Superprof, MyPrivateTutor, AmazingTalker, and TeacherOn dominate transactional keywords but operate as **unvetted marketplaces** where tutors compete on price
- No competitor combines (a) curriculum-specific tutor vetting + (b) transparent per-curriculum pricing + (c) detailed methodology per curriculum
- Competitor blog audits show zero content on "how to choose between IB and IGCSE for your child" or "what to expect in Year 11 of the British curriculum in Thailand"
- Market data: 77,700+ international school students in Thailand; these families need curriculum alignment, not cheap hourly rates
- Google rewards E-E-A-T: curriculum expertise signals (tutor qualifications, exam results, school partnerships) directly improve rankings

**What winning looks like:** Become the referenced source for curriculum-specific tutoring queries. Rank for "IB tutor Bangkok", "IGCSE tutor Phuket", "A-Level tutor Chiang Mai" and every curriculum x city combination within 5 months.

**How this plan delivers it:** Build 8 curriculum hub pages (Month 2). Build 30+ curriculum x location combination pages (Month 3-4). Publish comparison guides ("IB vs IGCSE in Thailand", "British vs American curriculum") (Month 4-5). Include structured data for Service schema with curriculum specificity.

---

### Bet 3: "SEN & Learning Support Tutors in Thailand — The Underserved Blue Ocean"

**Evidence:**
- **ADHD tutor Thailand** has only one real competitor: No Limits Community Services
- **Dyslexia tutor Bangkok** is virtually uncontested; OG-trained tutors are rare in Thailand
- BKK Kids lists 12+ learning support centres but most are clinics, not tutors
- SEN/ADHD support is the **most underserved blue ocean segment** across all research streams
- Keyword competition is "Low" for adhd tutor, dyslexia tutor, sen support bangkok, eal support bangkok, autism tutor thailand
- Competitors (TutorChase, Tutopiya, Krutoo) have **zero SEN-specific content or landing pages**

**What winning looks like:** Own the SEN tutoring keyword cluster entirely. Be the only tutoring service in Thailand with dedicated SEN pages, parent guides, and trained tutor credentials.

**How this plan delivers it:** Build 5 SEN-specific pages (ADHD tutor, dyslexia tutor, autism tutor, EAL support, general SEN support) in Month 3. Publish 2 SEN parent guides in Month 4. Add SEN-specialist structured data. Target partnerships with 3 international school learning support departments by Month 6.
