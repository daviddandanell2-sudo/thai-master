### Market Context

Thailand's international school sector has grown rapidly, with Bangkok alone hosting 91+ international schools across British, IB, American, and Australian curricula. Tuition fees range from 200,000 to 1,000,000+ THB/year, creating strong demand for supplementary tutoring. Private tutoring rates in Thailand typically range from 450–1,500 THB/hour, with premium IB/IGCSE specialists charging 2,000–3,700 THB/hour.

### SERP Insights

1. **Directories dominate transactional keywords**: Apprentus, Tutoroo, MyPrivateTutor, and TeacherOn consistently rank for "[subject] tutor [location]" patterns. A new domain must differentiate through specialized curriculum expertise and location depth.

2. **Service pages win for curriculum keywords**: ++tutors, Top IB Tutors, and Krutoo rank well for IB/IGCSE-specific terms with dedicated, content-rich service pages. Programmatic curriculum × location combination pages are essential.

3. **Blog content wins for informational keywords**: BKK Kids, 21K School, and Reloc8Asia dominate question-based searches with comprehensive guides. Long-form, authoritative content (2,000+ words) with tables and FAQs performs best.

4. **Individual tutor sites can rank for ultra-specific long-tail**: Samui Tutor (Sergey Rumyantsev) ranks for "A-Level math tutor Koh Samui" — demonstrating that niche + location depth beats broad authority for specific queries.

5. **Google rewards E-E-A-T signals**: Tutors with reviewer profiles, student testimonials, and curriculum-specific credentials rank higher. Pages should include tutor qualifications, student results, and school affiliations.

### Quick Win Strategy Notes

1. **Programmatic pages for SEN keywords**: Create dedicated `/subjects/adhd-support`, `/subjects/dyslexia-support`, and `/subjects/sen-support` pages with specialized tutor profiles and parent resources. The SEN market in Bangkok is underserved by general tutoring directories.
