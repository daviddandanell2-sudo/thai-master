# Location Subject Page Template

## Frontmatter

```yaml
---
title: "{Subject Name} Tutor in {Location Name}"
meta_description: "Find a qualified {subject} tutor in {Location}, Thailand. One-to-one {subject} tutoring for IB, IGCSE, A Level. In-person or online. WhatsApp us."
slug: "{location-slug}/{subject-slug}"
location: "{location-name}"
subject: "{subject-name}"
primary_keyword: "{subject} tutor {location}"
page_type: "location-subject"
word_count_target: "800-1200"
---
```

## Critical Rule

This page must be **genuinely unique**. It cannot be a simple merge of the location page and subject page. It must answer the specific question: **"Why does my child need a {subject} tutor specifically in {location}?"**

## Safe Combination Page Checklist

Every combination page MUST meet ALL of these criteria (from audit `08-GOOGLE-SEO-RULES.md` §2.2):

| # | Requirement | Minimum Standard | Fail Action |
|---|-------------|------------------|-------------|
| 1 | **Unique content percentage** | Minimum 60% of page content must be unique to that specific city-subject combination | Rewrite or noindex |
| 2 | **Word count** | Minimum 800 words per combination page | Expand content or noindex |
| 3 | **Unique sections required** | Location-specific intro, local context, subject-specific methodology, local pricing notes, local FAQ | Add missing sections |
| 4 | **Local signals** | Mention of 3+ specific local landmarks, schools, or educational context for that city | Add local context |
| 5 | **Internal links** | Links to 3+ other relevant combination pages + 1-2 blog posts | Add contextual links |
| 6 | **User value** | Clear answer to "why would someone in [city] searching for [subject] tutoring find this page more useful than the generic tutoring page?" | Reassess page purpose |
| 7 | **No auto-redirects** | Page must NOT redirect to a generic page - it must serve its own content | Fix or remove |
| 8 | **Template differentiation** | Maximum 40% of page structure can be shared across combination pages | Redesign template |
| 9 | **Local entity references** | Specific mentions of curriculum providers, international schools, or testing centers in that city | Add local references |
| 10 | **Contact/action** | Clear path to inquire about tutoring in that specific city/subject | Add city-specific CTA |

## Minimum Requirements Summary

- **Minimum 800 words** per combination page
- **60%+ unique content** requirement vs other combination pages
- **3+ local signals** (schools, landmarks, educational context)
- **3+ city-specific FAQ items** (not generic FAQs)
- **Internal links to 3+ related combo pages**
- **Template differentiation note**: Maximum 40% shared structure across pages

## Unique Angle Requirements

Before writing, identify at least one of these unique angles:
- Specific international schools in {location} that teach {subject} and their particular challenges
- Local exam centers or test prep needs in {location} for {subject}
- Expat community characteristics in {location} that affect {subject} learning
- Seasonal or local factors (e.g., "Families in Phuket often need math catch-up after long holidays")
- Specific curriculum-school combinations common in {location}

## Sections

### 1. Hero Headline (H1)

**Pattern:** "{Subject Name} Tutor in {Location Name}"

**Example:** "Math Tutor in Bangkok"

### 2. The Specific Problem in {Location}

**Purpose:** Explain why this subject in this location is unique.

**Pattern:**
> {Subject} tutoring in {Location} isn't just about finding someone who knows {subject}. It's about finding a tutor who understands:
> - The specific curricula taught in {Location}'s international schools
> - The pace and expectations of {Location}'s academic environment
> - The challenges faced by {Location}'s student population

**Example for Math in Bangkok:**
> Math tutoring in Bangkok requires more than just subject knowledge. Bangkok's international schools move quickly through the curriculum, and students often need help keeping pace. Whether your child is at Bangkok Patana working through IGCSE Extended Mathematics or at NIST tackling IB Math Analysis, a tutor who understands Bangkok's school landscape can make the difference.

**Rules:**
- 100-200 words
- Must include location-specific details
- Must not be copy-pasted from the generic subject or location page

### 3. Local Context for {Subject}

**Purpose:** Explain the local learning environment for this subject.

**Required elements:**
- Which schools in {location} teach this subject and at what level
- Common challenges students in {location} face with this subject
- Any local factors (language, teaching style, school culture) that affect learning

**Rules:**
- 100-200 words
- Specific to {location}
- Reference real schools where possible

### 4. How Tutoring Works in {Location}

**Purpose:** Explain the practicalities.

**Pattern:**
> Our {subject} tutors in {Location} offer:
> - In-person tutoring at your home in [specific neighborhoods]
> - Online tutoring for flexibility
> - Support for [specific curricula common in {location}]
> - Scheduling that works around [location-specific factors, e.g., "Bangkok traffic" or "Phuket's seasonal schedules"]

**Rules:**
- 100-200 words
- Mention practical location details

### 5. Trust Section

**Purpose:** Build confidence with local specificity.

**Pattern:**
> Parents in {Location} choose our {subject} tutors because:
> - [Trust signal specific to {location} and {subject}]
> - [Another trust signal]

**Rules:**
- 50-100 words
- Must be specific to this combination

### 6. FAQ (5 Questions, 3+ City-Specific)

**Required:**
1. "Do you offer {subject} tutoring in {location}?"
2. "Which curricula do your {location} {subject} tutors support?"
3. "Can the tutor come to our home in {location}?"
4. "How quickly can you match a {subject} tutor in {location}?"
5. "Do you offer online {subject} tutoring for {location} families?"

**At least 3 of these FAQs must contain city-specific details** (school names, local neighborhoods, curriculum specifics for that city, or local practicalities).

### 7. Call to Action

**Pattern:**
> Looking for a {subject} tutor in {location}? Message us on WhatsApp and we'll match you with a tutor who understands both {subject} and {location}'s schools.

### 8. Internal Links Block

**Pattern:**
> **Related:**
> - Tutoring in {Location}
> - {Subject} tutoring in Thailand
> - [Curriculum] {subject} tutor
> - [Another related page]

**Minimum 3 links to related combination pages** (e.g., other subjects in same city, same subject in other cities, curriculum-specific pages).
