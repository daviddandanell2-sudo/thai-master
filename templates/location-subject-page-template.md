## Critical Rule

This page must be **genuinely unique**. It cannot be a simple merge of the location page and subject page. It must answer the specific question: **"Why does my child need a {subject} tutor specifically in {location}?"**

## Unique Angle Requirements

Before writing, identify at least one of these unique angles:
- Specific international schools in {location} that teach {subject} and their particular challenges
- Local exam centers or test prep needs in {location} for {subject}
- Expat community characteristics in {location} that affect {subject} learning
- Seasonal or local factors (e.g., "Families in Phuket often need math catch-up after long holidays")
- Specific curriculum-school combinations common in {location}

## Sections

### 1. Hero Headline (H1)

**Pattern:** "{Subject Name} Tutor in {Location Name}"

**Example:** "Math Tutor in Bangkok"

### 2. The Specific Problem in {Location}

**Purpose:** Explain why this subject in this location is unique.

**Pattern:**
> {Subject} tutoring in {Location} isn't just about finding someone who knows {subject}. It's about finding a tutor who understands:
> - The specific curricula taught in {Location}'s international schools
> - The pace and expectations of {Location}'s academic environment
> - The challenges faced by {Location}'s student population

**Example for Math in Bangkok:**
> Math tutoring in Bangkok requires more than just subject knowledge. Bangkok's international schools move quickly through the curriculum, and students often need help keeping pace. Whether your child is at Bangkok Patana working through IGCSE Extended Mathematics or at NIST tackling IB Math Analysis, a tutor who understands Bangkok's school landscape can make the difference.

**Rules:**
- 100-200 words
- Must include location-specific details
- Must not be copy-pasted from the generic subject or location page

### 3. Local Context for {Subject}

**Purpose:** Explain the local learning environment for this subject.

**Required elements:**

- Common challenges students in {location} face with this subject
- Any local factors (language, teaching style, school culture) that affect learning

**Rules:**
- 100-200 words
- Specific to {location}
- Reference real schools where possible
