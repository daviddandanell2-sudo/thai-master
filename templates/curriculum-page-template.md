> - PYP (Primary Years Programme) — ages 3-12
> - MYP (Middle Years Programme) — ages 11-16
> - DP (Diploma Programme) — ages 16-19
>
> The IB is known for its inquiry-based approach, emphasis on critical thinking, and the requirement for students to complete the Extended Essay, Theory of Knowledge, and CAS (Creativity, Activity, Service) components.

**Rules:**
- 150-300 words
- Explain clearly for parents new to the curriculum
- No jargon without explanation
