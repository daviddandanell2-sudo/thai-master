## Sections

### 1. Hero Headline (H1)

**Pattern:** "Private Tutoring in {Location Name}"

**Example:** "Private Tutoring in Bangkok"

**Rules:**
- Include primary keyword
- Keep under 60 characters
- Be specific, not generic

### 2. Parent Problem Statement

**Purpose:** Acknowledge why the parent is here.

**Pattern:**
> Finding the right tutor in {Location} isn't always straightforward. Whether your child attends an international school, is transitioning between curricula, or needs extra support in a specific subject, you need a tutor who understands both the subject and the local context.

**Rules:**
- 2-4 sentences
- Show empathy
- Mention location naturally

### 3. Why {Location} Matters for Tutoring

**Purpose:** Explain the local context.

**Required elements:**
- Number of international schools in the area
- Common curricula used by families there
- Types of families living there (expat, local, digital nomad)
- What makes tutoring demand unique in this location

**Example for Bangkok:**
> Bangkok is home to over 50 international schools, making it the largest tutoring market in Thailand. Families here use a mix of IB, IGCSE, A Level, and American curricula. Many tutors in Bangkok specialize in specific curricula and have experience with the schools in Sukhumvit, Sathorn, and Thong Lor.

**Rules:**
- 100-200 words
- Specific details, not generic statements
- Reference real schools from `data/locations.json`

### 4. Subjects Available

**Purpose:** Show which subjects are covered in this location.

**Pattern:**
> We offer one-to-one tutoring in {Location} for:
> - Math tutoring in {Location}
> - English tutoring in {Location}
> - Science tutoring in {Location}
> - [Additional subjects relevant to this location]

**Rules:**
- Link each subject to the relevant subject page or location-subject page
- Only list subjects that make sense for this location's demand
- 50-100 words

### 5. Curricula Supported

**Purpose:** Show which curricula are covered.

**Pattern:**
> Our tutors in {Location} support students following:
> - IB (International Baccalaureate)
> - IGCSE (Cambridge International)
> - A Level
> - [Additional curricula relevant to this location]

**Rules:**
- Link each curriculum to the relevant curriculum page
- Mention specific schools that use each curriculum
- 50-100 words

### 6. Tutor Matching Process

**Purpose:** Explain how tutors are selected for this location.

**Pattern:**
> When you request a tutor in {Location}, we:
> 1. Understand your child's subjects, curriculum, and goals
> 2. Match you with a tutor who knows {Location}'s schools and curriculum
> 3. Arrange a trial session to confirm the fit
> 4. Provide ongoing support and progress updates

**Rules:**
- 100-200 words
- Mention location-specific advantages (e.g., "tutors who know Bangkok's traffic patterns and can reach Sukhumvit easily")

### 7. Trust Section

**Purpose:** Build confidence.

**Pattern:**
> Parents in {Location} trust us because:

- Specific to the location

### 8. FAQ (5-7 Questions)

**Required questions:**
1. "Do you offer online tutoring in {Location}?"
2. "Which curricula do your {Location} tutors support?"
3. "How quickly can you match a tutor in {Location}?"
4. "Do your tutors come to our home in {Location}?"

**Optional questions:**
6. "How much does tutoring cost in {Location}?"
7. "Can you help with exam preparation in {Location}?"

**Rules:**
- Short, direct answers (50-100 words each)
- Link to related pages where relevant

**Rules:**
- Specific to the location
- Low-pressure
- Clear next step
